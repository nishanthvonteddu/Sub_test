    What the product gives you

    One workspace for monthly spend, renewal timing, and lifecycle dates.

    The product is designed for utility first: what you pay, when it renews, when it started, and when it ends.

    Monthly visibility

    See baseline recurring spend and upcoming monthly charges together.

    The forecast view compresses the next several months into a readable spending rhythm instead of scattered reminders.

    Renewal timing

    Read the exact dates plans renew and the month they land in.

    The renewal board groups charges by month, making timing easy to scan before subscriptions compound into noise.

    Lifecycle history

    Track when subscriptions started, whether they paused, and when they end.

    Active, paused, and canceled plans all stay visible in the ledger so spend history remains legible.
